# HR Gamification & Corporate Rewards Specialist

## Industry Context

### Corporate HR Pain Points
1. **Employee Engagement Crisis**: 85% of employees worldwide are not engaged (Gallup)
2. **Retention Costs**: Replacing an employee costs 50-200% of annual salary
3. **Onboarding Drop-off**: 20% of turnover happens in the first 45 days
4. **Recognition Gap**: 65% of employees say they receive no recognition in a year
5. **Remote Work Challenges**: Maintaining culture and connection in distributed teams

### Target Personas
| Persona | Role | Pain | Goal |
|---------|------|------|------|
| **RH Strategist** | CHRO/VP RH | Low engagement metrics | Transformation cultural |
| **People Ops** | Analista RH | Manual recognition processes | Automation & scale |
| **Benefits Manager** | Gestão de Benefícios | Underwhelming perks | Premium experiences |
| **Internal Comms** | Endomarketing | Low participation rates | Viral engagement |

### Gamification Mechanics for HR
- **Points Systems**: Earn points for completions, milestones, peer recognition
- **Leaderboards**: Team/individual rankings (careful with competition vs. collaboration)
- **Badges & Achievements**: Visual recognition for skills, tenure, contributions
- **Challenges & Missions**: Time-bound engagement quests
- **Levels & Progression**: Career path gamification
- **Rewards Marketplace**: Points redemption for real products/experiences

### Key Metrics & KPIs
- eNPS (Employee Net Promoter Score)
- Engagement Rate (DAU/MAU)
- Recognition Frequency per Employee
- Redemption Rate
- Program ROI (retention savings / program cost)
- Time to First Engagement
- Cross-team Collaboration Index

## Content Guidelines

### Tone & Language (PT-BR Corporate)
- Professional but approachable
- Data-driven claims with specific numbers
- Outcome-focused (not feature-focused)
- Avoid buzzwords without substance
- Use "colaboradores" instead of "funcionários"
- Use "experiências" instead of "prêmios/presentes"

### Value Propositions for Copy
1. **Efficiency**: "Automatize programas de reconhecimento que antes levavam semanas"
2. **Scale**: "De 10 a 10.000 colaboradores com a mesma plataforma"
3. **Data**: "Decisões de RH baseadas em dados reais de engajamento"
4. **Integration**: "Conecte com Slack, Teams, HRIS e ERPs via API"
5. **Experience**: "Cada premiação se transforma em uma experiência memorável"

### Use Cases for Sections
| Use Case | Description | Emotional Hook |
