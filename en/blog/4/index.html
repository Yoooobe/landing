Gamified welcome kit: onboarding people will talk about

Pair a physical welcome box with digital missions on 4unik in the first 30 days — structured, human onboarding.

First physical touch teaches culture. Add 4unik missions (mentor coffee, training, first 1:1) for rhythm without HR overload.

Platform · landing

Onboarding and employee journey

The Platform overview ties missions, recognition and the store — the right frame to ask for help shaping month one.

Open Platform page

Suggested first-30-day missions

  • Complete profile and key policies.
  • Schedule coffee with the direct manager.
  • Join a team ritual.
  • Give first peer feedback on the internal tool.

Pair physical kit and digital journey

Put a QR or link to the first 4unik mission inside the welcome kit — physical gesture pulls people into the digital habit. Keep the tone welcoming, not bureaucratic.

How 4unik helps in this scenario

Rewards store

Rewards from day one

The store page explains catalog and redemption — pair with welcome kits and paths; 4unik reps help you tune it.

View rewards store
  • Mission paths by period (7, 15, 30 days) with points and visible recognition.
  • Rewards store to redeem points for items that reinforce belonging (books, experiences, kit upgrades).
  • Dedicated new-hire campaigns, separate from the rest of the company.
  • Integrations to sync audience with HRIS and reduce manual errors.

Award points for meaningful steps — not an endless checklist. Few missions with real meaning beat twenty trivial tasks.
