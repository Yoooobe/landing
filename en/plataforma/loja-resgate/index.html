Platform · Corporate Store

The reward store where employees choose their own prize.

A fully white-labeled storefront connected to your points program. Employees browse the catalog, redeem points, and receive physical or digital rewards — without any manual work from HR.

Catalog
Unlimited

Physical products, digital items, experiences, and branded kits.

Checkout
Automated

Points deducted automatically; shipping calculated at order time.

Delivery
Global

4Unik fulfillment or API integration with your own ERP and carrier.

What the store delivers

Configurable catalog

Add physical products, digital vouchers, branded kits, or experiences. Filter by category, tag, or campaign to control what each employee sees.

Gift wizard for managers

HR defines a gift (name, products, recipients) in four steps and schedules delivery. No spreadsheets, no emails — just click and ship.

Points-only checkout

Employees pay entirely with their earned balance. Optional mixed payment (points + money) can be enabled per campaign.

Integrated logistics

Track every order from the admin panel. Use 4Unik fulfillment or connect your ERP and carrier stack via API.

Product variants and SKUs

Sizes, colors, and bundles are handled natively. Stock levels and availability update in real time.

Segmentation and campaigns

Restrict catalog items to specific employee groups or campaigns. Control what is visible, when, and to whom.

How a redemption works

01

Earn points

Recognition events (shoutouts, badges, goals) credit the employee's balance automatically.

02

Browse the store

Employee opens the branded store, filters the catalog, and reads product details.

03

Complete checkout

Points are deducted at checkout; address and shipping method are confirmed.

04

Receive the reward

The item ships to the employee's door via 4Unik fulfillment or your logistics partner.

The store in action

Product pages show price in points, stock, description, and delivery conditions.

The cart shows item breakdown, subtotal in points, and shipping estimate.

Managers review the gift before saving. Scheduling is optional.
