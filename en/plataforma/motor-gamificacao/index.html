What the engine delivers

Campaigns for real business actions

Model hackathons, internal challenges, onboarding flows, incentive pushes, and recurring engagement programs with their own timing, audience, page, and rewards.

Native engine or external integrations

Use the native 4unik gamification system or connect external engagement platforms, CRM, HRIS, and other event sources through APIs, webhooks, and manual actions.

Corporate store as the redemption layer

Each campaign can publish its own redemption page with curated products for employees, teams, and partners, with auditable points usage.

Messaging and operations in one flow

Landing page, banner, wallet, notifications, and logistics stay aligned so campaigns move from launch to delivery without fragmented operations.

How 4unik runs a campaign

01

1. Create the campaign and redemption page

Define the objective, target audience, and the dedicated experience for hackathons, recognition actions, internal challenges, or goal-based programs.

02

2. Configure rules and messaging

Set identity, communication, timing, and point rules so participants understand the action and how rewards will work.

03

3. Open the catalog and distribute value

Attach products, limits, and distribution logic so participants can earn benefits and redeem through the corporate store.

04

4. Track redemptions through delivery

Managers monitor adoption, balances, orders, and fulfillment while 4unik supports the operation through the final delivery step.
