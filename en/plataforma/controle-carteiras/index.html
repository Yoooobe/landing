Wallet control

Participants track points, redemptions, and balance in one auditable wallet.

In 4unik, employees and campaign participants can review balances, check point history, follow redemptions, and use points inside the corporate store with full traceability. The wallet can also receive data from external gamification tools while keeping point usage auditable from credit to redemption.

Live balance
Instant visibility

Participants see balances and point movements as soon as campaigns, integrations, and adjustments are processed.

Traceability
100%

Every credit, debit, and redemption shows the reason, type, date, and resulting balance.

Journey integration
Wallet + store

The wallet stays connected to the catalog, orders, and external engagement systems in one auditable flow.

What the wallet solves

Clear participant view

Employees and campaign participants can track current balance, total earned, total spent, and transaction history in a familiar interface.

Points tied to campaigns and actions

4unik records campaign bonuses, recognition points, goal achievements, order usage, and operational adjustments with full traceability.

Auditable point usage

Managers can audit the full path from credit to redemption, validate policies, and eliminate manual reconciliation work.

Store and external tool integrations

The wallet connects to the catalog, checkout, orders, and external gamification tools that need to send or consume points.

How the wallet operates day to day

01

1. Earn points in the right moment

Points enter through campaigns, goal completion, recognition, external integrations, or manual adjustments with clear attribution.

02

2. Review wallet and statement

Participants check balances, point history, and the origin of each credit before using them.

03

3. Redeem through the corporate store

Points are used on products and experiences in the store, with an auditable debit connected directly to the order.

04

4. Track orders and history

Participants and managers follow the order, the statement, and redemption status while operations maintain governance end to end.
